<h4>The purpose of Physical Education is to develop physically competent and confident individuals who enjoy a lifetime of active and healthy living safely and responsibly.</h4>
<p>The five goals outlined below provide a framework for our Physical Education
curriculum, each playing a crucial role in developing students' understanding,
skills, practices, and values.</p>
<p><strong>Movement Competence</strong>
<br>Students are competent and confident to participate in a range of physical
and outdoor activities.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 80%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="PE1" src="/images/Departments/PE/PE1.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Healthy Lifestyle Practices</strong>
<br>Students have a personal commitment to healthy lifestyle practices in
physical activity, nutrition, sleep, outdoor time and hygiene.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 80%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="PE2" src="/images/Departments/PE/PE2.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Safety Mindset</strong>
<br>Students apply risk assessment to manage daily and physical activities
with respect to self, others and the environment.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 80%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="PE3" src="/images/Departments/PE/PE3.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Core Values</strong>
<br>Students make informed and responsible decisions with regard to personal
behaviour and social interactions based on sound values-based judgements.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 80%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="PE4" src="/images/Departments/PE/PE4.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Enjoyment</strong>
<br>Students enjoy and value physical activities and healthy living in a sustainable
way</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 80%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="PE5" src="/images/Departments/PE/PE5.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Our goal is for students to adopt a lifetime of active and healthy living by:</strong>
<br>- Engaging in regular physical activity and minimizing sedentary time
<br>- Enjoying the outdoors responsibly
<br>- Making balanced dietary choices with a focus on healthier options
<br>- Prioritizing sufficient rest and sleep
<br>- Practicing good personal hygiene</p>
