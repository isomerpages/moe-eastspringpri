<p><strong>The purpose of PE in East Spring Primary School is to develop physically competent and confident individuals who enjoy a lifetime of active and healthy living.</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>The five goals outlined below provide a framework
for our PE curriculum, with each goal playing a crucial role in fostering
students’ understanding, skills, practices, and values.</p>
<p><strong>Movement Competence</strong>
</p>
<p>Students are competent and confident to participate in a range of physical
and outdoor activities.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Movement Competence" src="/images/Departments/PE/PE1.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Healthy Lifestyle Practices<br></strong>Students have a personal
commitment to healthy lifestyle practices in physical activity, nutrition,
sleep, outdoor time, and hygiene.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Healthy Lifestyle Practices" src="/images/Departments/PE/PE2.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Safety Mindset</strong>
<br>Students apply risk assessment to manage daily and physical activities
with respect to self, others and the environment.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Safety Mindset" src="/images/Departments/PE/PE3.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Core Values</strong>
<br>Students make informed and responsible decisions with regard to personal
behaviour and social interactions based on sound values-based judgements.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Core Values" src="/images/Departments/PE/PE4.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Enjoyment</strong>
<br>Students enjoy and value physical activities and healthy living in a sustainable
way</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Enjoyment" src="/images/Departments/PE/PE5.png">
</div>
<p><strong>Our goal is for students to adopt a lifetime of active and healthy living by:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Engaging in regular physical activity and minimizing sedentary time</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enjoying the outdoors responsibly</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Making balanced dietary choices with a focus on healthier options</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Prioritizing sufficient rest and sleep</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Practicing good personal hygiene</p>
</li>
</ul>
