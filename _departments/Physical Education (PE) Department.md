<p><strong>The purpose of PE in East Spring Primary School is to develop physically competent and confident individuals who enjoy a lifetime of active and healthy living.</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p>The five goals outlined below provide a framework for our PE curriculum,
with each goal playing a crucial role in fostering students’ understanding,
skills, practices, and values.</p>
<p><strong>Movement Competence</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Students are competent and confident to participate in a range of physical and outdoor activities.</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Healthy Lifestyle Practices<br>Students have a personal commitment to healthy lifestyle practices in physical activity, nutrition, sleep, outdoor time, and hygiene.</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Safety Mindset<br>Students apply risk assessment to manage daily and physical activities with respect to self, others and the environment.</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Core Values<br>Students make informed and responsible decisions with regard to personal behaviour and social interactions based on sound values-based judgements.</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Enjoyment<br>Students enjoy and value physical activities and healthy living in a sustainable way</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Our goal is for students to adopt a lifetime of active and healthy living by:</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>- Engaging in regular physical activity and minimizing sedentary time</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>- Enjoying the outdoors responsibly</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>- Making balanced dietary choices with a focus on healthier options</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>- Prioritizing sufficient rest and sleep</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>- Practicing good personal hygiene</strong>
</p>
