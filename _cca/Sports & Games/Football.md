<p>The objective of the club is to provide an avenue for students to enjoy
playing football while developing their understanding of the sport and
building their character through their involvement in the sport.</p>
<p>Students are categorised into three groups:</p>
<p><strong>Recreational (Group 1)<br></strong>Members in the recreational
team are students who wish to focus on learning the foundation skills of
the sport and enjoy playing small-sided tactical games to expand their
understanding of the sport in a supportive environment.</p>
<p><strong>School Development Team (Group 2)</strong>
<br>Members learn the foundation skills of the sport and practise core skills
in their game. Selected players who are equipped with a solid grasp of
core skills will advance to the school team for more intensive and structured
training. Players participate in football drills that require decision-making
skills and engage in intensive football games to expand their understanding
of the sport. Players are exposed to friendly matches, football carnivals,
and different levels of tournaments including zonal and national competitions.</p>
<p><strong>School Team (Group 3)</strong>
<br>Members in the school team are selected from the Development Team and
Recreational groups to form teams of U9, U10, U11 and U12. Players participate
in football drills that require decision-making skills and engage in intensive
football games to expand their understanding of the sport. Players are
exposed to friendly matches, football carnivals, and different levels of
tournaments including zonal and national competitions.</p>
<p><strong>Character Building</strong> 
<br>Students focus on developing teamwork, discipline and sportsmanship alongside
their football abilities. The programme emphasises that football activities
instill values of perseverance and respect for teammates and opponents.
Members gain leadership skills and learn the importance of commitment through
regular training and competitive experiences.</p>
