<p>The objective of the club is to provide an avenue for students to enjoy
outdoor activities while developing their understanding of essential life
skills and building their character through their involvement in scouting
programmes.</p>
<p>Students participate in a programme that includes:</p>
<p><strong>Skills Development</strong> 
<br>Members learn the foundation skills of scouting and practise essential
survival techniques including map reading, knotting, outdoor cooking and
camping. Students develop practical abilities through hands-on and adventure-packed
activities. Players participate in diverse activities that help them master
crucial outdoor skills and expand their understanding of self-reliance
and teamwork.</p>
<p><strong>Community Service</strong> 
<br>Students are exposed to meaningful community involvement projects where
they can make a positive impact by helping others. These activities provide
valuable experience and help students put their values and skills into
action. Members learn the importance of service and develop a sense of
responsibility towards their community.</p>
<p><strong>Character Building</strong> 
<br>Students focus on developing strength of character and independence alongside
their practical abilities. The programme emphasises that scouting activities
instil resilience and compassion.</p>
