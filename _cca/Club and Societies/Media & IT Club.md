<p>The objective of the club is to provide an avenue for students to enjoy
digital creativity and technology while developing their understanding
of modern digital skills and building their character through their involvement
in media and information technology.</p>
<p>Students participate in a programme that includes:</p>
<p><strong>Skills Development</strong> 
<br>Members learn the foundation skills of digital media and practise photography,
videography, coding and audio-visual operations. Students develop technical
abilities through hands-on experience with AV equipment and digital tools.
Players participate in activities that help them master ICT skills and
expand their understanding of digital media knowledge while becoming digitally
fluent.</p>
<p><strong>Leadership Opportunities</strong> 
<br>Students are exposed to school events and activities where they can apply
their skills by capturing and documenting important moments. These experiences
provide valuable opportunities and help students develop strong leadership
abilities. Members learn the importance of responsibility through operating
technical equipment and develop confidence in managing digital projects.</p>
<p><strong>Character Building</strong> 
<br>Students focus on developing leadership skills and technical competence
alongside their digital abilities. The programme emphasises that media
and IT activities empower students to take charge and make positive contributions.
Members gain practical skills that enable them to showcase their talents
and make a meaningful impact on the school community.</p>
