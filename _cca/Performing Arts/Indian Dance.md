<p>The objective of the club is to provide an avenue for students to enjoy
learning Indian dance while developing their understanding of various Indian
dance styles and building their character through their involvement in
traditional and contemporary dance.</p>
<p>Students participate in a programme that includes:</p>
<p><strong>Skills Development</strong> 
<br>Members learn the foundation skills of Indian dance and practise various
styles ranging from classical Bharatha Natyam to energetic Bollywood and
Folk Dance. Students develop dance abilities through exposure to a wide
spectrum of techniques. Players participate in activities that help them
master different Indian dance forms and expand their understanding of this
rich art form.</p>
<p><strong>Performance Opportunities</strong> 
<br>Students are exposed to school and external showcases including the Singapore
Youth Festival where they can demonstrate their skills. These events provide
valuable experience and help students grow in confidence through performances.
Members learn the importance of teamwork through ensemble dancing and develop
stage presence that captivates audiences.</p>
<p><strong>Character Building</strong> 
<br>Students focus on developing confidence and dedication alongside their
dance abilities. The programme emphasises that regular practise builds
lasting passion for Indian dance. Members gain cultural appreciation and
artistic growth through their involvement in this diverse and dynamic art
form.</p>
