<p>The objective of the club is to provide an avenue for students to enjoy
exploring music while developing their understanding of musical instruments
and building their character through their involvement in music.</p>
<p>Students participate in a programme that includes:</p>
<p><strong>Skills Development</strong> 
<br>Members learn the foundation skills of various musical instruments and
practise essential techniques. Students develop musical abilities through
hands-on learning experiences. Players participate in activities that help
them master different instruments and expand their understanding of music.</p>
<p><strong>Performance Opportunities</strong> 
<br>Students are exposed to school concerts where they can showcase their
musical talents. These events provide valuable experience and help students
grow in confidence. Members learn the importance of teamwork through group
performances and develop stage presence.</p>
<p><strong>Character Building</strong> 
<br>Students focus on developing confidence and teamwork alongside their musical
abilities. The programme emphasises that music fosters deep appreciation
for the arts. Members gain skills that nurture their creative expression
and help them find their musical voice for lifelong enjoyment.</p>
